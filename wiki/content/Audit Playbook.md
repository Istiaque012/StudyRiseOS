# Audit Playbook

Three repeatable audit types.

## 1. Monthly Health Check
- GSC: impressions, clicks, CTR trends
- GA4: traffic sources, AI-referral numbers
- Google Trends: keyword seasonality shifts
- Near-miss keywords (position 8-20) — quick wins
- Technical spot-check: orphans, thumbnails, sitemap, robots.txt

## 2. AI-Visibility Check
- Query brand name in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini
- Track citation presence/absence
- GA4 AI-referral segment analysis

## 3. Targeted On-Demand
- Specific page deep-dive when performance drops
- Competitor comparison on specific keywords
- index.html root check (ensure not serving app shell)

## Maintenance Mindset
Rankings decay without maintenance. Freshness signals matter. Every audit should produce action items filed into [[Content Pipeline]] or [[Internal Link Strategy]].
