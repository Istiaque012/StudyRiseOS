# Help Center

Public, indexable product docs at `/help/*` on the website — a **first-class content type,
separate from the blog** (never in blog feeds, hubs, or category listings). Spec lives in
`raw/brain-v1/13_HELP_CENTER.md`; execution plan in `EXAM_HELP_CENTER_BUILD_PLAN.md`.

## Exam Wave 1 — LIVE 2026-07-02 (PR #61 merged to `main`, deployed)

18 pages: `/help` home (router, 3 mode cards), `/help/exam` hub (CollectionPage mapping all
articles under six headings), and 15 articles giving **complete Exam Mode coverage**:

- **Getting started:** getting-started (★ cornerstone) · build-your-plan · subjects-and-tasks
- **Daily loop:** daily-study-session · spaced-repetition · logging-questions · mock-exams
- **Manage & adjust:** plan-and-schedule · staying-on-track
- **Track progress:** dashboard · readiness-and-projections · history · analytics-and-reports
- **Final month & config:** revision-sprint · settings — plus **faq**

## Key conventions

- Articles are `TechArticle` + `BreadcrumbList` (+ `HowTo`/`FAQPage` with schema↔visible parity);
  every `<h2>` opens with a 40–60 word direct answer then numbered steps.
- All illustrations are **HTML/CSS mocks** (persona Dr. Sarah Chen, mid-plan; no screenshots);
  only raster asset is the shared OG card `help-og.webp` — ⚠️ still to be generated by Istiaque
  (§6.3 prompt in the build plan).
- Product facts come only from the app repo's `EXAM_MODE_TUTORIAL.md`; exam facts (pass marks,
  subject counts) stay in the dedicated exam content and are linked out.
- Pro features (Analytics, AI Study Advisor, Go/No-Go, Revision Sprint) named plainly
  ("This is part of StudyRise Pro."); pricing copy per [[Pricing Model]].
- Hub-and-spoke linking per [[Internal Link Strategy]]; all help URLs in `sitemap.xml`.
