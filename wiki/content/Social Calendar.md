# Social Calendar

Timing layer for social operations. Defines when to post, how to promote articles, and which seasonal moments to build around — broken down by audience segment. Companion to [[Social Playbook]] and [[Social Content Formats]].

## Article Echo Protocol

Every blog article gets a structured social promotion sequence after publishing.

### 7-Step Sequence

| Timing | What | Template | Platforms |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 0** | Launch announcement | ST1 + text post | All four |
| **Day 1** | Key insight pull | S1 or S3 | IG, FB |
| **Day 3** | Carousel or short video | C1 or V2 | IG, YT Shorts |
| **Day 5** | Group share | F1 | FB groups |
| **Day 7** | Video companion | V1 or V3 | YT, IG Reels |
| **Day 14** | Pull-quote or secondary insight | S1 or S2 | IG, LinkedIn |
| **Day 30+** | Evergreen reshare | Text post, fresh angle | FB, LinkedIn |

### Echo Decision Tree
- **Flagship/high-priority cluster**: Full 7-step echo (Day 0–30+)
- **Segment-specific**: Abbreviated — Day 0, 3, 5 on that segment's platforms only
- **Everything else**: Minimum — Day 0 + Day 3

### Day 0 Launch Specifics
- **Facebook**: Title + 2–3 sentence excerpt + article URL with UTM
- **Instagram**: ST1 story sequence (hook → stat → OG image → link sticker)
- **YouTube**: Short or community post with key insight + link
- **LinkedIn**: L2/L3 professional-angle post. Links in body OK for launch posts.

## Weekly Posting Grid

| Day | Facebook | Instagram | YouTube | LinkedIn |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | S3/S1 | V2/C1 Reel | — | L1/L2 |
| Tue | — | Story | — | — |
| Wed | Article/F1 | S1/S2/S4 | V2/V3 Short | — |
| Thu | — | Story (echo) | — | L3 |
| Fri | Community/reshare | C1/C2/C3 | — | — |
| Sat | — | V1/V4 Reel | V1/V4 Short | — |
| Sun | Batch-create | — | Long-form (if ready) | — |

### Batching Rules
- Sunday/Monday: Write all captions, prepare image prompts
- One recording session per week for all Reels/Shorts
- Never create and post same day (except Day 0 launches)

### Posting Windows (audience local time)
- Facebook: 9–11 AM, 7–9 PM
- Instagram: 7–9 AM, 11 AM–1 PM
- YouTube: Upload anytime (Shorts by 10 AM)
- LinkedIn: 8–10 AM weekdays only

Multi-timezone defaults: Bangladesh time for [MBBS-BD], Australian morning for [EXAM] AMC, 9 AM UTC for [ALL].

## Segment Calendar — [EXAM] AMC MCQ

AMC MCQ is year-round (Feb–Nov, Pearson VUE). No fixed deadlines — calendar is cycle-driven, not event-driven.

### AMC Preparation Arc

| Stage | Months Out | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Decision** | 6+ | Pathway overview, eligibility |
| **Planning** | 4–6 | Build plan, resource selection, daily targets |
| **Execution** | 2–4 | Study technique, weak-area management, progress |
| **Final Stretch** | 0–2 | Mock exams, logistics, mental prep |
| **Post-Exam** | After | Results, next steps, retake strategy |

Rotate through all stages monthly so followers at any stage find relevant content. 12-month theme calendar in source doc §3.2.

### AMC Rules
- Never guess pass rates, fees, or format changes — verify against official AMC site, include "as of [Month Year]"
- Clinical exam content parked until StudyRise supports Clinical Mode
- Facebook IMG groups are high-value — share weekly with F1

## Segment Calendar — [MBBS-BD] Bangladesh

Anchored to **May and November professional examinations** (BMDC cycle).

### Key Phases
- **Jan–Apr**: Teaching period → pre-exam awareness → exam prep
- **May**: 🔴 Professional exams — reduce posting to 1–2/week, no product CTAs, maximum restraint
- **Jun**: Results + new phase planning
- **Jul–Oct**: Teaching period → pre-exam awareness
- **Nov**: 🔴 Professional exams — same restraint as May
- **Dec**: Break/results, light content only

### MBBS-BD Rules
- Restrained tone every month — no gamification, no pressure framing
- During exam months: purely supportive posts only, no marketing
- Supplementary exam content requires extreme sensitivity — factual, never shaming
- BMDC facts from FEATURES.md only — flag `[VERIFY: BMDC source needed]` otherwise
- Facebook college groups are primary discovery — F1 with full tone compliance
- **Never imply Pro is needed to pass** — ethical floor is free

### BMDC Event Triggers
Professional exam dates announced → eligibility check reminder. Exam week → reduce/stop posting. Results → interpretation guide. New session → phase setup walkthrough. Supplementary window → factual prep guidance with maximum sensitivity.

## Segment Calendar — [UNI] University

Dual-hemisphere model: Northern (Jan–May spring, Sep–Dec fall) and Southern (Feb–Jun Sem 1, Aug–Nov Sem 2).

### Key Rules
- Instagram is primary platform; YouTube Shorts for long-tail search
- LinkedIn low priority for this segment
- Hemisphere-sensitive: acknowledge both cycles in posts, or post two versions
- Assignment/deadline content is high-value and high-save-rate
- Tone: lightest of three segments, peer-level, no grind culture

## Paid Promotion Triggers

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| Flagship article published | Boost Day 0 post, $5–10/day for 5 days |
| Organic post high engagement | Boost it, $5–10/day for 3 days |
| Exam season approaching | Traffic campaign to segment landing/article |
| Product milestone | Consider conversion campaign |
| Content gap (trending topic, no article) | Write article first, then promote |

### When NOT to Promote
- During MBBS exam weeks (May, Nov)
- Product has known bugs/outages
- Content violates [MBBS-BD] restrained tone
- Article live < 24 hours

## Content Repurposing

Monthly reshare audit (1st of each month):
1. Top 3 posts from last 90 days — reshare with fresh angle?
2. Top blog articles by traffic — promoted in last 30 days?
3. Seasonal relevance check against monthly themes

Rules: never repost identical content, minimum 6-week gap, verify facts still current.

## When Not to Post

- MBBS exam weeks → max 1/week, no CTAs
- Product outage → pause promotional content
- National mourning/crisis → pause all posting
- Exhaustion → skip the day (consistency = weekly, not daily)
- Istiaque unavailable → don't post [MBBS-BD] without review
