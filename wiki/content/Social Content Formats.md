# Social Content Formats

Library of 21 reusable post templates for every platform and format. Each template includes structure, worked example, audience/pillar tags, funnel stage, and an AI image generation prompt. Companion to [[Social Playbook]].

**Video constraint:** Istiaque does not appear on camera. All video templates use screen recordings, voiceover-over-visuals, animated text, or motion graphics.

## Template Index

### Part 1 ‚Äî Static Image Posts

| Template | Name | Platforms | Pillar | Funnel |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **S1** | Single-Stat Insight | FB, IG (feed), LinkedIn | Study Technique / Exam Strategy | Awareness |
| **S2** | Myth vs. Fact | IG (feed), FB, LinkedIn | Study Technique | Awareness |
| **S3** | Quick Tip Card | IG (feed), FB | Study Technique | Awareness |
| **S4** | Before/After | IG (feed), FB | Product in Action | Consideration |

## Article-to-Social Cheat Sheet

| Article Type | Facebook | Instagram | YouTube | LinkedIn |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Study technique guide | F1 + S3 | C1 + V2 | V2 (Short) | L2 |
| Exam strategy article | F1 + S1 | C1 + V4 | V3 or V4 | L2 or L3 |
| Product walkthrough | S4 | V1 (Reel) | V5 + V1 (Short) | Skip or L1 |
| Comparison / listicle | S2 | C2 or C3 | V3 (Short) | L2 |
| MBBS-BD specific | F1 (BD groups) | Skip | V1 or V5 | Skip |

## Design Constants

- All templates use brand palette: Navy #0D1B3E, Purple #7C3AED, Cyan #06B6D4, White
- Typography: Inter (ExtraBold for stats, Bold for headings, Regular for body)
- Logo placement: bottom-right or bottom-centre, always small
- No stock photos, no face-on-camera, no clickbait visual language
- See [[Image Protocol]] and [[Brand Visual System]] for full specs
