# Brand Voice

**Tagline:** Plan Today. Rise Tomorrow.

## Five Voice Pillars
1. **Structured** — Clear, organized, methodical
2. **Momentum-Driven** — Progress-focused, forward motion
3. **Trustworthy** — Reliable, no hype, evidence-based
4. **Quietly Ambitious** — Confident without being loud
5. **Intelligent** — Smart without being condescending

## Per-Audience Voice
- **[EXAM]** — Focused, strategic, countdown-aware
- **[UNI]** — Balanced, practical, semester-oriented
- **[MBBS-BD]** — Formal, BMDC-authoritative, NO gamification

## Banned Phrases
See [[02_BRAND_VOICE]] §7.2 for the full banned-phrases list

## Approved Alternatives
structured, evidence-based, built for, designed around, your progress, your plan

## Copy Rules by Surface
- Landing pages: benefit-led, concise
- Blog articles: helpful-expert tone, direct-answer openings
- Social: approachable but never casual — see [[Social Playbook]] for per-platform register adaptation
- In-app: functional, clear, zero fluff

## Social-Specific Banned Phrases
In addition to the main banned list, these are banned in social copy: "Link in bio" (as entire CTA), "Drop a 🔥 if you agree", "Tag someone who needs this", "No one is talking about this", "This changed everything", "STOP scrolling". Full social voice rules in [[Social Playbook]].
