# Pricing Strategy

## Model
Freemium with 30-day full access onboarding → Free Starter or Pro.

## Per-Mode Pricing

### Exam Mode [EXAM] — USD
- Monthly, quarterly, or one-time options
- Stripe payments

### MBBS Bangladesh [MBBS-BD] — BDT
- Local pricing in Bangladeshi Taka
- bKash, Nagad, Rocket payment methods
- Ethical-floor features always free (core BMDC tracking)

### University Mode [UNI] — BDT/USD
- Dual-tier: Bangladesh (BDT) and Global (USD)

## Key Rules
- Data never deleted — downgrade restricts features, not data
- No "free trial" language — it's "30-day full access"
- Batch/campus channel available for institutions
- Ambassador rewards program

## Banned Copy Phrases
"premium", "unlock", "free trial", "revolutionary", "hack" — see [[Brand Voice]]
